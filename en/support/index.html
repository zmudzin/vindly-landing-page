Technical Support

App stops working after screen timeout.

Your device manufacturer (e.g., Xiaomi, Samsung) aggressively kills background processes. Lock the app in the recent tasks view and check dontkillmyapp.com.

Navigation Bridge does not restore the map.

Do not swipe Vindly away from the recent apps list. This action completely breaks the monitoring intent and halts the navigation routing.

I cannot see the music panel.

The Audio Node is disabled in the Free plan. An active Premium subscription is required.
