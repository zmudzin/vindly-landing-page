Frequently Asked Questions

How do I block notifications while driving on Android?

Vindly is a smartphone overlay that automatically suppresses non-critical alerts during active navigation. Unlike standard Do Not Disturb modes, it allows navigation prompts and important calls through. Available on Google Play.

Is there a HUD app for motorcycle navigation?

Yes. Vindly renders a minimal overlay (Ghost UI) on top of any navigation app on your smartphone. It is a dedicated phone app, not a replacement for built-in vehicle screens. Tested on motorcycle mounts in harsh rain and heavy vibration.

How to reduce phone screen glare while driving at night?

After sunset, the system automatically activates the Amber Horizon profile. It shifts the interface colors to a warm palette, protecting your vision in the dark and reducing eye fatigue. Premium plan feature.

Can I use my phone as a car HUD without distractions?

Vindly transforms any Android smartphone into a safe cockpit instrument. In Stealth Mode, the screen remains quiet and interface elements only appear when an event requires your immediate attention. No additional hardware required.

How to stop sensory overload and digital noise while driving?
